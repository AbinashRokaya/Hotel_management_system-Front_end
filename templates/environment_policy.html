At ARHOTEL

we are dedicated to promoting sustainable and eco-friendly practices in the hospitality industry.

  1. Commitment to Sustainability
    • We use energy-efficient systems for hosting and operations to reduce our carbon footprint.
    • Our booking processes are paperless to minimize waste.
  2. Resource Management
    • We support recycling and encourage minimal resource consumption wherever possible.
    • Our platform is designed to help hotels adopt sustainable practices, such as digital management tools.
  3. Liability
    • While we strive to provide accurate and reliable information, ARHOTEL is not responsible for errors, inaccuracies, or interruptions in service.
    • We are not liable for damages resulting from your use of our website.
  4. Advocacy for Green Hospitality
    • We partner with eco-conscious vendors and encourage our clients to embrace sustainable business models.
    • As part of our mission, we aim to raise awareness about the importance of environmental responsibility in the hospitality industry.
